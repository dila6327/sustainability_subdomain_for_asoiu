Sustainable Development Research Center

Advancing knowledge, innovation, and practical solutions for sustainable development at Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU)

About the Research Center

The Sustainable Development Research Center at ASOIU is dedicated to advancing knowledge, innovation, and practical solutions for sustainable development. The center operates with full commitment to advancing the center's research, educational initiatives, and community engagement efforts through dedicated Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) staff.

Research Focus Areas

Cutting-Edge Research

Conducting innovative research in renewable energy, climate change mitigation, sustainable urban development, and water resource management with collaboration across faculties and disciplines to develop innovative solutions.

Training Programs

Offering specialized training programs, workshops, and seminars for students, researchers, and professionals to enhance knowledge and skills in sustainability-related topics. Building future leaders in sustainable development.

Policy Development

Collaborating with governmental and non-governmental organizations to develop and promote policies aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Providing evidence-based recommendations to decision-makers.

International Collaboration

Establishing collaborations with international research institutions, universities, and sustainability-focused organizations to exchange knowledge, best practices, and funding opportunities for joint projects.

Green Startups

Supporting startups and projects that focus on sustainable technologies and eco-friendly solutions. Providing mentorship and guidance to students and researchers aiming to commercialize their innovations.

Education Integration

Contributing to undergraduate and postgraduate programs by integrating sustainability topics into the curriculum. Equipping students with knowledge and skills needed for careers in sustainable development.

Dedicated FTE Staff

Having dedicated Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) staff committed to advancing the center's research, educational initiatives, and community engagement efforts. Our specialized staff ensures that the center maintains high standards and provides expert support for all projects.

ECOFFIN: Sustainable Paraffin Innovation Project

A groundbreaking collaborative project addressing CO2 emissions from rocket launches through sustainable paraffin technology development.

Project Overview

A group of students from ASOIU's UFAZ and ADA University teamed up with international researchers to develop the Sustainable Paraffin project (CODAGE). This initiative directly addresses two important UN SDGs:

  • SDG 7: Affordable Clean Energy
  • SDG 12: Responsible Production and Consumption

Key Team Members:

  • Elgun Hasanov (ASOIU Department of Chemical Engineering)
  • Emin Khalifayev (ASOIU Department of Chemical Engineering)
  • Turan Ahmadov (ADA University)
  • Ismayil Huseynov (ADA University)

Technology & Impact:

The solution uses a patented reaction catalysis combined with ultrasonic wave treatment. This innovative process results in a sustainable paraffin that reduces CO₂ emissions by an impressive 27% - a significant advancement toward addressing the alarming amount of CO₂ emitted by rocket launches (300 tonnes per launch, with 2,500 launches expected in the next decade).
