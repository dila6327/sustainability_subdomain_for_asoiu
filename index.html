Frequently Asked Questions

ASOIU is committed to integrating sustainability across all aspects of university operations, from research and education to campus management and community engagement. We aim to be a leader in sustainability innovation and practice.
Students can participate through various programs including research projects, student clubs, community service initiatives, and sustainability-focused courses. Contact the Sustainability Office for more information.
The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are 17 global goals for sustainable development adopted by the UN. ASOIU aligns its research, education, and operations with these goals to contribute to global sustainability.
We use various metrics and assessment tools including carbon footprint analysis, waste reduction tracking, energy efficiency monitoring, and participation in international sustainability rankings.
ASOIU offers numerous research opportunities in areas such as renewable energy, green technology, environmental science, sustainable development, and climate action. Visit our Research page for more details.
You can reach the Sustainability Office through our website contact form, email at sustainability@asoiu.edu.az, or visit us in person at the main campus office during business hours.
