5
Sustainable Development Goal

Gender Equality

Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU) is firmly committed to promoting Sustainable Development Goal 5 – Gender Equality, which seeks to achieve equality and empower all women and girls.

Key Objectives

1

Measure and track women's application rate and acceptance or entry rate

2

Women's access schemes such as mentoring, scholarships or other provisions

3

Accessible childcare facilities for students to attend university courses

4

Childcare facilities for staff and faculty

5

Women's mentoring schemes with at least 10% female student participation

6

Monitoring women's likelihood of graduating and closing the gap

7

Equal opportunity in employment and advancement

8

Safe campus environment with anti-harassment measures

9

Gender-aware curriculum and awareness programs

10

Female leadership and decision-making participation

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Gender equality policies and support programs
  • Women's empowerment initiatives and mentorship
  • Equal opportunity in admissions and employment
  • Research on gender studies and women's rights
