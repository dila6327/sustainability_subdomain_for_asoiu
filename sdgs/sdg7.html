7
Sustainable Development Goal

Affordable and Clean Energy

ASOIU is a leader in energy research, focusing on transitioning to renewable energy sources, enhancing energy efficiency in campus infrastructure, and developing innovative green technologies.

Key Objectives

1

Increase the share of renewable energy in campus electricity consumption

2

Implementation of energy-efficient lighting (LED) and HVAC systems

3

Promotion of energy conservation practices among students and staff

4

Investment in research for solar, wind, and hydrogen energy

5

Regular energy audits to identify and fix energy leakages

6

Development of smart grid technologies within the university

7

Collaboration with industry for clean energy innovations

8

Public outreach on energy transition and sustainability

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Installation of solar panels on university buildings
  • Creation of the Center for Renewable Energy Research
  • Energy efficiency training for technical staff
  • Partnership with SOCAR and international energy firms
