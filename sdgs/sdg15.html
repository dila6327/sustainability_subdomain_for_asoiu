Sustainable Development Goal

Life on Land

ASOIU is committed to protecting, restoring and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

Key Objectives

1

Conservation and restoration of terrestrial ecosystems

2

End of deforestation and restoration of forests

3

Combat desertification and land degradation

4

Prevention of species extinction

5

Elimination of poaching and trafficking

6

Integration of ecosystem values in planning

7

Prevention of invasive species

8

Research on biodiversity

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Terrestrial ecology and biodiversity research
  • Reforestation and habitat restoration projects
  • Wildlife conservation and protection initiatives
  • Land management and sustainable agriculture studies
