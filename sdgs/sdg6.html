6
Sustainable Development Goal

Clean Water and Sanitation

ASOIU ensures sustainable management of water resources and sanitation through efficient water usage, wastewater treatment, and providing access to clean drinking water across the campus.

Key Objectives

1

Free drinking water provided for students, staff and visitors

2

Water-efficient building standards and fixtures across campus

3

Wastewater treatment and sustainable disposal systems

4

Water reuse and recycling programs for landscaping

5

Regular monitoring of water quality and consumption

6

Promoting water conservation awareness among students

7

Support for local community water management projects

8

Research in water desalination and purification technologies

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Campus-wide installation of water filtration stations
  • Modernization of plumbing for water efficiency
  • Industrial wastewater research projects
  • Public awareness workshops on "Blue Gold" conservation
