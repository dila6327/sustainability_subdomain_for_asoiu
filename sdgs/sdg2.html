Sustainable Development Goal

Zero Hunger

ASOIU is committed to ending hunger and achieving food security by ensuring access to affordable, nutritious food for all students and staff through subsidized meals, food vouchers, and sustainable sourcing practices.

Key Objectives

1

Affordable campus canteens and subsidized meal plans for low-income students

2

Food voucher programs and emergency meal support

3

Awareness campaigns on healthy and sustainable eating

4

Partnerships with local food suppliers emphasizing sustainable sourcing

5

Reduction of food waste from university food service

6

Prevention of student food insecurity and hunger

7

Sustainable food choices for all on campus

8

Healthy and affordable food choices for all staff members

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Healthy Life Initiative and Plastic-Free Week events
  • Student Affairs Department food security coordination
  • Nutritional support and meal planning services
  • Sustainable food sourcing and local partnerships
