Sustainable Development Goal

Climate Action

ASOIU takes urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts through greenhouse gas emissions reduction and climate resilience building.

Key Objectives

1

Strengthen climate change resilience and adaptive capacity

2

Integration of climate change mitigation into policies

3

Education and awareness on climate change

4

Reduction of greenhouse gas emissions

5

Stabilization of greenhouse gas concentrations

6

Implementation of climate action policies

7

Support for least developed countries

8

Research on climate science and adaptation

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Climate Action Plan with carbon neutrality targets
  • Campus greenhouse gas emissions reduction
  • Climate research and environmental science programs
  • Sustainable energy transition and renewable adoption
