Sustainable Development Goal

Reduced Inequalities

ASOIU is dedicated to reducing inequalities by ensuring inclusive access to education, promoting diversity in our community, and protecting the rights of all students and staff regardless of background.

Key Objectives

1

Track and support students from low-income families

2

Ensure non-discrimination policies for all protected groups

3

Increase the proportion of first-generation students entering university

4

Provide accessible facilities for people with disabilities

5

Promote scholarship schemes for underrepresented minorities

6

Implement measures to close the graduation gap for disadvantaged students

7

Empower female and minority leadership within the administration

8

Foster a culture of respect and global citizenship on campus

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Inclusive admissions and targeted support for low-income students
  • Disability support services and infrastructure accessibility
  • Global exchange programs for diverse student populations
  • Anti-discrimination training for faculty and staff
