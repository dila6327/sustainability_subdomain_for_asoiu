Sustainable Development Goal

No Poverty

ASOIU is committed to ending poverty in all its forms by supporting financially disadvantaged students and fostering economic opportunities within the local community.

Key Objectives

1

Support for students from the bottom 20% income group

2

Graduation completion targets for low-income students

3

Financial assistance programs

4

Community entrepreneurship support

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Student financial aid programs
  • Scholarship schemes for underprivileged students
  • Entrepreneurship support for local community
  • Community development initiatives
