Sustainable Development Goal

Good Health and Well-being

ASOIU promotes physical, mental, and social well-being for all students, staff, and the wider community through healthcare services, wellness programs, and mental health support.

Key Objectives

1

On-campus medical points and basic health consultations

2

Preventive healthcare awareness among students and staff

3

Regular health check-ups and vaccination campaigns

4

Collaborations with health institutions to improve well-being outcomes

5

Outreach programs in the local community for health promotion

6

Sharing sports facilities with local schools and the general public

7

Students access to sexual and reproductive health-care services

8

Students with access to mental health support

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Health and Wellness Services
  • Mental Health Support Programs
  • Community Health Outreach
  • Sports and Wellness Facilities
