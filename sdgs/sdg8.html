8
Sustainable Development Goal

Decent Work and Economic Growth

ASOIU fosters sustainable economic growth by providing quality employment, supporting entrepreneurship, and ensuring a safe and productive working environment for all staff and students.

Key Objectives

1

Ensure fair recruitment and decent working conditions for all staff

2

Support student entrepreneurship and start-up incubation

3

Zero tolerance for forced labor, modern slavery, and child labor

4

Maintain a safe and secure working environment for all employees

5

Guarantee equal pay for work of equal value regardless of gender

6

Protect labor rights and promote collective bargaining

7

Provide career guidance and placement services for graduates

8

Foster economic innovation through industry-academia partnerships

ASOIU's Initiatives & Objectives

  • Employment and labor rights monitoring
  • Entrepreneurship and innovation hub (e-ASOIU)
  • Career center and graduate employability programs
  • Corporate social responsibility in procurement
