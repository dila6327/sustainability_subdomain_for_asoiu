Seminar on “Ethical and Transparent Communication: Building Trust, Balancing Facts and Emotion” Held at ASOIU

The “Public Speaking and Communication Skills” session at Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University featured Zamira Adilova, who delivered a training on ethical and transparent communication, emphasizing trust-building, legal frameworks, and accurate information sharing in public institutions. Through practical examples and an interactive “Chain of Trust” exercise, students explored how easily information can be distorted and the importance of maintaining clarity and reliability in communication.

Second Training Session Held Within the “Public Speaking and Communication Skills” Project at ASOIU

The “Public Speaking and Communication Skills” session at the Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU), led by Khulya Sabirli, focused on structuring informative speeches, audience engagement, and classical rhetoric principles including logos, ethos, and pathos. Participants gained practical skills in preparing fact-based presentations, delivering content effectively, and received guidance on maintaining objectivity, with top performers set to be recognized and awarded at the program’s conclusion.

Training on "Emotional Intelligence" held at ASOIU within the "IDEALISE" incubation program

The "Emotional Intelligence" training at Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU), part of the "IDEALISE" Incubation Program supported by the Innovation and Digital Development Agency (IDDA), was led by Aysel Sadigova from ASOIU's Startup and Technology Transfer Office. Participants explored concepts like empathy, conflict management, and team dynamics, completing practical exercises that highlighted emotional intelligence as a key factor for startup success.

Training on "Building an effective and innovative organizational structure" held at ASOIU

On October 24, AZI Academy was launched at the Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU) to promote a culture of learning, development, and sharing, with its first training on "Building an effective and innovative organizational structure" conducted by Rector Associate Professor Rufat Azizov. The session emphasized the dual approach of maintaining efficiency while exploring innovation, aiming to enhance leadership skills, management culture, and the professional potential of university employees.

ASOIU becomes a permanent member of the Global University Network for Innovation

Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU) has become a permanent member of the Global University Network for Innovation (GUNi), joining 363 institutions from over 100 countries. This membership positions ASOIU as Azerbaijan’s sole representative in the network, enhancing its international standing and fostering innovative academic and social initiatives.

Acting Rector of ASOIU meets with Family Members of Martyr Graduate

On September 12, Acting Rector Associate Professor Vazeh Askarov of Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU) met with the family of martyr graduate Ibrahim Jafarov, emphasizing the university’s efforts to honor its 23 martyr graduates through memorials and named auditoriums.
