Office of Disability Services (ODS)

The Office of Disability Services at ASOIU is dedicated to ensuring equal access to university life by removing physical and systematic barriers for students and staff with disabilities.

Accessibility Note: ODS operates under the strategic oversight of the EDI Office to manage Individual Accessibility Plans (IAP) and ensure all university facilities meet universal design standards.

Core Responsibilities & Services

  • Individual Accessibility Plans (IAP): Coordinating personalized support and academic adjustments for students.
  • Inclusive Housing: Managing requests for accessible rooms and reasonable modifications in university residences like Univerium.
  • Reasonable Adjustments: Approving practical measures and assistive technologies to ensure full participation in campus activities.
  • Service Animal Approval: Reviewing and approving requests for service or assistance animals for students on campus.
  • Institutional Advocacy: Advising the Sustainability Committee on facility upgrades and funding allocations for accessibility.
