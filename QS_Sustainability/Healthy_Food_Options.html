Healthy and Affordable Food Options on Campus

ASOIU prioritizes the health and well-being of its entire community—students, faculty, and staff—by ensuring access to nutritious, safe, and affordable food options on campus. In alignment with SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) and SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), the university has institutionalized sustainable food policies that balance quality, affordability, and accessibility.

Commitment to Health and Sustainability

Key Actions
  • Introduction of balanced menus designed by nutrition consultants
  • Promotion of water and non-sugary drinks over carbonated beverages
  • Waste minimization via reusable serving materials
Survey Result (2024)
86%
Rated food affordability and quality as "good" or "very good"

Impact & Conclusion

ASOIU actively provides and maintains healthy, and affordable food options for everyone on campus. This initiative supports the physical and mental well-being of students while reinforcing the university's contribution to SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), and SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production).
